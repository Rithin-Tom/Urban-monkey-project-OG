Oversized Hoodies FAQs

What is the best place to buy women's hoodies?

One of the best places to buy hoodies for women is Urban Monkey. We have an exclusive collection of Tie and Dye unisex Oversized hoodies that will make you look stylish and casual. You can team it up with the fantastic Airplane Mode sunglasses and a pair of jeans and walk like a fashionista.

How often should I wash my hoodies?

All hoodies from Urban Monkey, including cotton hoodies and men's street hoodies, come with a care label for washing. All our hoodies are machine washable. Just make sure you use cold water. For more details, please check the label and wash your cool hoodies according to the instructions.

1. What is the best colour to get when buying a hoodie?

Tie and dye are currently trending in hoodies for men and women. They represent peace carried by free-spirited people. If you enjoy wearing cool hoodies, go with neon hues or a combination of purple and pink to flaunt your style statement.

2. What is my hoodie made of? What is it lined with?

Your cute hoodies for girls and stylish hoodies for men use 100% cotton fabric. These are soft, breathable, and have a relaxed fit. You also have an adjustable drawstring hood, extra pouch pocket, and fitted trims so that you never go wrong with your hoodies.

3. How to choose your hoodie Size?

Check out the Urban Monkey size chart to pick the perfect size for you. If you want oversized hoodie streetwear, then go for one size larger than your size.

4. What is the difference between a hoodie and a sweatshirt?

It is simple. A sweatshirt is a long-sleeved pullover shirt, while a hoodie has the same features with an extra hood.
